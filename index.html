Jacaranda tree

Jacaranda tree or Jacaranda mimosifolia, is a tree that grows in subtropical zones native to South America.
The distinct colour of it's flowers, that ranges between purples, made it popular around the globe
It's why you can see it outside it's native enviorment in any part of the world

The flowers of the tree get their enchanting violet color from Anthocyanin.
Which is water-soluble pigment found in a wide variety of flowers, plants, seeds and more!
Depending on their pH, their color can range from purple, to red, to blue and even black.
They only flower during spring and summer

The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall.
And while it can't survive frost.
Mature trees can withstand temperatures of around -7 C° for brief periods of time.
